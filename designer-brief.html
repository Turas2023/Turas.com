Project

Website Homepage – Turas (Healthcare Recruiting)

Brand Objective

Create a calm, premium, and trustworthy homepage communicating long-term partnership and healthcare expertise.

Target Audience

Hospital administrators, clinic owners, healthcare professionals.

Brand Personality

Calm, professional, ethical, enduring, relationship-focused.

Visual Direction

Deep green primary, ivory background, muted gold accent. Serif or clean sans-serif fonts. Strong white space.

Do Not Use

Bright colors, gradients, buzzwords, heavy stock imagery, aggressive CTAs.

Success Feels Like

A firm trusted with long-term, important decisions.

Primary CTA

Partner With Us / Request a Consultation

Implementation Notes

Keep the layout minimal. One primary CTA per section. No sliders, heavy animations, or aggressive effects.
